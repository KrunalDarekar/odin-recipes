Dosa

Description

Dosa is the ever-popular South Indian breakfast of crispy crepes made with fermented rice and lentil batter. With just a handful of ingredients learn to make the classic Dosa recipe from scratch with my easy video and step-by-step photos. I also cover making your own dosa batter in a blender or mixer-grinder, tips on fermentation and cooking dosa to help you make the best dosa crispy, soft and so good to dunk in a bowl of Coconut Chutney or piping hot Sambar.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad dal

Steps

  1. Soak rice and lentils in two separate bowl
  2. Grind or blend the rice and lintils till a soft batter forms
  3. Add one tablespoon of table salt and let the batter ferment for 8-10 hours
  4. Butter a non-stick pan
  5. Take the batter in a semi spherical spoon
  6. Spread it on the pan with bottom of the spoon
  7. Wait till bubbles appear on the surface then flip the dosa
  8. Wait for another 20 seconds and your dosa is ready
  9. Serve with sambar and mint chutney
